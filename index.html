About the Author

Jamal Nazir Samaan is an independent author and researcher whose work explores the intersection of geopolitics, religion, historical narrative, and cultural memory in the Middle East. His writings analyze sacred geography, political symbolism, and the evolving structures of global power, with particular focus on Syria and the broader Middle Eastern region.
